Little Story

Kumari Beauty Center & Institute is ideally located in the southern part of Sri Lanka. Kumari Beauty Center & Institute is where you can get all the services you need such as birthday cake, wedding cake, cake structure, curtain, mosquito net, children's and women's dress sewing and courses. Established in 2002, Kumari Beauty Center & Institute has been steadily growing for 24 years now.
